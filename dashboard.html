Paneer Butter Masala

Vegetarian Contains Dairy, Cashew

Delicious North Indian dish made with paneer in a rich, creamy tomato sauce.

Paneer Butter Masala

INGREDIENTS

Paneer - 1 slice

Salt - 2.5 Tsp

Chilli Powder - 1.5 Tsp

Cream - 10 ml

Milk - 1 Cup

Cashew - 5 gms

Onion - 2 Bulbs

Garlic - 2

Ginger - 2 Shreds

Coriander - Garnish

RECIPE

1. Boil water and cook veggies, then blend them.

2. Gold fry the paneer.

3. Add blended mixture to the paneer.

4. Pour cream and milk, then simmer.

5. Add cashew paste for creaminess.

6. Let it cook for 15 minutes.

7. Garnish with coriander leaves.

8. Serve hot with naan or rice.

9. Enjoy your dish!

10. Adjust spices as needed.

Chicken Biryani

Non-Vegetarian Contains Nuts, Ghee

Aromatic and flavorful, this chicken biryani will make your taste buds dance!

Chicken Biriyani

INGREDIENTS

Basmati Rice - 1/2 Cup

Chicken - 100 gms

Onions - 1 Small

Tomatoes - 1/2 Small

Yogurt or Curd - 2 Spoons

Ginger-Garlic paste - 1 Tsp

Biriyani Masala - 1 Tsp

Saffron - A pinch

Mint and Coriander - Garnish

Ghee 1 Tsp

RECIPE

1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, and salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes.

2. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then boil with salt and whole spices (cardamom, cloves, bay leaf) until 70% cooked and drain.

3. Thinly slice onions and fry in hot oil until golden and crispy.

4. Soak saffron in warm milk.

5. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee, add marinated chicken, and cook until tender and the masala thickens.

6. In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer the cooked chicken at the bottom, then top with partially cooked rice.

7. Sprinkle fried onions, chopped mint and coriander leaves, and drizzle saffron milk on top.

8. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid or seal with dough. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.

9. Let the biryani rest for 10 minutes before opening.

10.Gently fluff up the rice with a fork and serve hot with raita or a side salad.

Steamy Momos

Vegetarian Contains Mushroom, Soy

Healthy stir fry made with fresh veggies and a tangy sauce.

Steamy Momos

INGREDIENTS

Flour (Maida) - 1 cup

Mushroom - 50 grams

Cabbage - 2 Tsp

Carrot - 1 Tsp

Onion - 1 Tsp

Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1/2 Tsp

Soy Sauce - 1/2 Tsp

Salt - 1/2 Tsp

Pepper - 1/4 Tsp

Water - As needed for dough

RECIPE

1. Mix flour with a pinch of salt and water. Knead into a soft dough and set aside.

2. Combine minced filling, cabbage, carrot, onion, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.

3. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each into thin circles.

4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle. Fold and pleat the edges to seal.

5. Grease the steamer tray lightly or line with cabbage leaves.

6. Arrange momos on the tray, ensuring they don’t touch.

7. Bring water to a boil in the steamer base.

8. Place the tray inside, cover, and steam for 10-12 minutes.

9. When the outer covering becomes glossy and slightly translucent, they are done.

10.Carefully take out the momos using tongs and serve hot with a spicy dipping sauce or chutney.

Grilled Prawns

Non-Vegetarian Contains Prawns

Simple and tasty grilled prawns sauted with honey chilli sauce.

Grilled Prawns

INGREDIENTS

Prawns - 150 grams

Olive Oil - 1 Tsp

Lemon Juice - 1 Tsp

Garlic - 1 clove

Paprika - 1/4 Tsp

Salt - 1/2 Tsp

Black Pepper - 1/4 Tsp

Fresh Parsley - 1 Tsp

Soy Sauce - 1/2 Tsp

Honey - 1/2 Tsp

RECIPE

1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, salt, pepper, soy sauce, and honey to make the marinade.

2. Add the prawns to the marinade and toss to coat well.

3. Let the prawns marinate for at least 15 minutes.

4. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

5. Thread the prawns onto skewers (if using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes beforehand).

6. Place the prawns on the grill.

7. Grill the prawns for about 2-3 minutes on each side, until they turn pink and opaque.

8. Remove the prawns from the grill.

9. Sprinkle with fresh parsley.

10.Serve hot with a lemon wedge on the side.

Chicken Croissants

Non-Vegetarian Contains Dairy, Nuts, Soy, Gluten

An Italian cuisine with a twist of cheesy shredded chicken with some delicious seasoning.

Chicken Croissants

INGREDIENTS

Croissant Dough: 1 pre-made dough

Cooked Chicken: 100 grams

Mayonnaise: 1 Tsp

Dijon Mustard: 1 Tsp

Salt: 1/4 Tsp

Black Pepper: 1/4 Tsp

Cheddar Cheese: 1 Slice

Egg Wash: 1 Beaten egg

Fresh Lettuce: 1 Leaf

Tomato: 2 Slices

RECIPE

1. Preheat the oven according to croissant dough package instructions.

2. Roll out the croissant dough and cut it into a triangle shape.

3. In a bowl, mix shredded chicken with mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper.

4. Place a spoonful of the chicken mixture on the wide end of the dough triangle.

5. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of the chicken mixture.

6. Roll the dough from the wide end towards the point, tucking in the sides.

7. Brush the croissant with egg wash.

8. Place the rolled croissants on a baking sheet.

9. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and flaky.

10.Once cooled slightly, slice open and add fresh lettuce and tomato.

Potato wedges

Vegetarian Contains Wheat starch, Dairy

Golden roasted potatoes coated with dragon bbq honey sauce.

Potato Wedges

INGREDIENTS

Potatoes: 1 large, cut into wedges

Olive Oil: 1 Tsp

Salt: 1/2 Tsp

Black Pepper: 1/4 Tsp

Paprika: 1/4 Tsp

Garlic Powder: 1/4 Tsp

Onion Powder: 1/4 Tsp

Dried Rosemary: 1/4 Tsp

Parmesan Cheese: 1 Tsp grated

Parsley: 1 Tsp

RECIPE

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).

2. In a bowl, mix olive oil with salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and dried rosemary.

3. Toss potato wedges in the seasoned oil mixture until well coated.

4. Arrange the wedges in a single layer on a baking sheet.

5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top if using.

6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, turning once halfway through, until golden and crispy.

7. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly.

8. Garnish with chopped parsley.

9. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

10.Enjoy your delicious and crispy potato wedges!
